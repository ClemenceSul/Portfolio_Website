Phoenix Farms

Fully self-sufficient, Phoenix Farm offers an innovative solution to cultivate crops efficiently and sustainably in some of the harshest environments on Earth. Tent structures protect the crops, with a fleet of DARTs (robotic entities) handling various tasks, using the resources available in the central unit.

In Depth Research

This project was centered around analysing what the world would look like in 30 years, and finding solutions to mitigate problems in 2042. As such, substantial research was done to piece together the future scenario and identify the tech enablers that would be available then.

System Design

The solution proposed is the implementation of a fully autonomous agricultural system. Thus, the overall functionality of the system was primodial in all ideation sessions and I evaluated each part of Phoenix Farms to ensure they would work with the other subsystems.

COO Role

In my group of 5, I took on the role of a COO during this project. I organised all the group meetings, notes, timeline, and coordinated the work between all other group members.
