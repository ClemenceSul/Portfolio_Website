Discover Windows

In older generations, computer illiteracy causes disparities, isolation or dependancy. Following user research, I created this webapp as an easy interactive guide to give the elderly the necessary tools to communicate, understand and use their computers.

Discover Windows

I discovered seniors understood their computer’s interface best with visual prompts which mirrors their computers so I decided to recreate these windows. Furthermore, I noticed seniors often don’t click on unknown buttons, wary of causing an unwanted reaction. Hence all ‘buttons’ in my webapp were launched when they were hovered upon.
