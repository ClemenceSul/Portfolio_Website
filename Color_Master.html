Color Master

A sassy opponent

Autumn 2021

Mastermind is a simple logic game in which the user attempts to find the hidden code through multiple iterations. Play against the Color Master, as he removes the wrongly placed blocks but keeps those rightly placed until you crack the code, or lose!

Programmed Arduino Mega 2560 with C++

Design validation with CAD

Color sensors and motorized arms assembly

Prototyping & Laser cutting plywood
