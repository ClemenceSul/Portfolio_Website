Flying Mouse

An on-going project

Autumn 2022 - ...

A regular mouse requires contact with a surface in order to detect movement. However, this can become quite unpractical when working on small surfaces or presenting. Hence why I’m attempting to create a mouse which would work in the air.

First prototype with lego technic pieces : left & right button with joystick to move mouse.
Failed attempt with Arduino Mega 2650 to control computer mouse as it's not compatible with HID.
Successful control of computer mouse with an Arduino Pro Micro.
Modelled with clay around hand (long time to dry) - model too constrictive of knuckle movement.
Modelled with papier mache around hand (long time to dry) - uneffective result.
Modelled with layered cardboard - quite constrictive of the index, uncomfortable.
Modelled with layered cardboard - hard to reach joystick.
Sufficient modelled with layered cardboard - to refine with components' real size.
Failed connection of Pro Micro to PC via HC-05 Bluetooth module.
Successful attempt with Bluefruit Feather 32u4 with the integrated bluetooth, when labeled as a keyboard or a computer.
